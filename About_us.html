About us

This site is created by 4 first-year students of American University of Armenia majoring in Computer Science. This site is the result of our huge interest towards Cryptography. In our website, we have collected the best available resources and problems about Cryptography. What makes this site unique is that we have created programs which give our users an opportunity to encrypt and decrypt any word, phrase or even a whole paragraph using their preferred cipher.

In future we plan:

1. To increase the security of the website by making the inspect code unavailable for users.

2. Enrich the amount of ciphers.

3. Create encryption and decryption programs for all available ciphers.

When encrypting, a person looks up each letter of the message in the "plain" line and writes down the corresponding letter in the "cipher" line.
